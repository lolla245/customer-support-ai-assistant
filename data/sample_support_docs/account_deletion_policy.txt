Account Deletion Policy
==============================

You have the right to delete your account at any time. Please read the following before proceeding.

What happens when you delete your account:
- All personal data associated with your account will be permanently removed.
- Your order history, saved addresses, and preferences will be deleted.
- Any unused wallet balance or gift cards will be forfeited.
- Active subscriptions will be cancelled immediately (no refunds for remaining period).

How to Delete Your Account:
1. Log in and go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
2. Read the confirmation prompt carefully.
3. Enter your password to confirm identity.
4. Click "Delete My Account".

Your account will be scheduled for deletion within 7 days. During this period, you can cancel the deletion by logging back in.

After 7 days:
- Account is permanently deleted.
- Data is removed from our servers within 30 days as per our Privacy Policy.
- This action cannot be undone.

Note: If you have any pending orders, please wait for them to be fulfilled or cancelled before deleting your account.

To initiate account deletion via support: privacy@example.com
