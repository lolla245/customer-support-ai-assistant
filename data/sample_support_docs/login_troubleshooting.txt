Login Troubleshooting Guide
==============================

Having trouble logging in? Here are the most common issues and how to fix them.

Issue 1: Incorrect Password
- Make sure Caps Lock is off.
- Try typing your password in a text editor first to verify it.
- Click "Forgot Password?" to reset it.

Issue 2: Account Not Found
- Double-check the email address you are using.
- You may have signed up with a different email (e.g., Gmail vs. work email).
- Try searching your inbox for a welcome email from us to confirm the registered address.

Issue 3: Account Locked
- After 5 failed login attempts, your account is locked for 15 minutes.
- Wait and try again, or reset your password.

Issue 4: Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Problems
- Make sure your authenticator app time is synced correctly.
- If you lost access to your 2FA device, use a backup code from your account setup.
- If you don't have backup codes, contact support with proof of identity.

Issue 5: Browser or App Issues
- Clear your browser cache and cookies.
- Try logging in on a different browser or device.
- Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers) temporarily.

Issue 6: Social Login (Google / Facebook) Not Working
- Make sure you're using the correct social account linked to your profile.
- Try logging in with email and password instead.

Still can't log in? Contact support: support@example.com
