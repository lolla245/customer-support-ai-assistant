How to Reset Your Password
==============================

If you've forgotten your password or need to reset it, follow these steps:

Step 1: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?"
Step 2: Enter the email address associated with your account.
Step 3: Check your inbox for a password reset email (it may take up to 2 minutes).
Step 4: Click the link in the email — it is valid for 30 minutes only.
Step 5: Enter your new password twice to confirm.

Password Requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number or special character

Troubleshooting:
- Didn't receive the email? Check your spam/junk folder.
- Link expired? Request a new one from the login page.
- Still locked out? Contact support at support@example.com
