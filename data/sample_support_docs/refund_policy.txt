Refund Policy
==============================

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Here is our refund policy:

Eligibility:
- Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.
- The item must be unused, in its original condition, and in original packaging.
- Digital products are non-refundable once downloaded or accessed.

How to Request a Refund:
1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders".
2. Select the order and click "Request Refund".
3. Choose the reason for the refund and submit.
4. Our team will review your request within 2–3 business days.

Refund Processing Time:
- Approved refunds are credited back to the original payment method.
- Credit/Debit cards: 5–7 business days.
- Wallet balance: within 24 hours.

Non-Refundable Items:
- Gift cards
- Shipping charges
- Items marked as "Final Sale"

For any refund disputes, please email billing@example.com
